Target Audience

The targeted audience for this website is a very wide audience ranging from young adults to elderly. Specifically to the local areas in which the weather is being forcasted. Incomes include all poverty level up to upper-class citizens. The audience includes all categories of marital status, education level, etc. All demographics are targeted.

Persona 1

Landon Q. Reynolds

  • Age: 25
  • Income: $30,000 yearly
  • Education: Some college
  • Experience: Consists of checking the local weather on mobile device. Occasionally viewing the weather on television.
  • Marital Status: Single
  • How, When, and Where accessing site: Landon checks the weather first thing every morning on his mobil device in hopes that it is cool enough to wear his scarf from france, although at times it makes people quesiton his sexuality.
