Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help individuals living in the local areas to find the current or upcoming forcast, read about local weather news, and find or submit gallery photos pertaining to the local weather. Anyone who wishes may have access to contact information to Predictable Weather Corp. This content of this site is to be easily accessible to all who wish to have access.
