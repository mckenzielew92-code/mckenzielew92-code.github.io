Temple Highlights

Newport Beach California Temple

The Newport temple was announced in April of 2001, and was dedicated in August of 2005. Members of the Church of Jesus Christ living particularly in Orange County were pleased to have a temple that they could get to within the hour. Prior to the Newport Beach Temple being built, the closest temple was either the Los Angeles, or the San Diego temple. Both temples being about a 2 hour drive one way. Having one close makes it easier for local families to make frequent visits and they couldn't be more grateful to have multiple temples so close to them.

The Newport temple had to be a shade of light tan because of it's location. The city would not let a white temple be built as it would not fit in the community. In order to build a temple the church agreed to paint the outside to work with the local city's color scheme.
