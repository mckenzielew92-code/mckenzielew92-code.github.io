Visual Hierarchy

World Market

World Market used the visual hierarchy design principle well in their site. You can see that the thing that draws your eye is the: 50% off rugs sale! They accomplish this by making the box stand out against everything else with a bright red color and making the box very large.

Contrast

Old Navy

Old Navy did a good job at exemplifying the contrast design principle. The contrasting colors they chose were gold, white, navy blue, and a torquoise green. When put side by side it's easily read and compliment eachother.

Visit Old Navy

Hick's Law

Disneyland

Disney uses the Hick's Law design principle by making their site very plain and simple as soon as it is pulled up by the browser. There is only a few links to click on and not much to read. It makes the decision to buy tickets easy with the button easily visible and accessible.
