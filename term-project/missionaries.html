Missionary Suites

If you are serving a mission in the area, check out or long-term housing options for senior missionaries. These suites are our top of the line suites. We want you to be able to come home to a cozy, comfortable abode after a long days work serving to lord in building his kingdom. Our missionary suites are fully equiped with an in suite kitchenette, including a stove, oven, microwave, dishwasher and refridgerator. Missionary suites also include a mini washer and dryer in the master bedroom closet. Each missionary suite also includes an office for any work that needs to be done. Select suites come with a balcony with amazing views. These suites are perfect for a temperary home. We hope you choose us to help make these two coming years, the best years!
