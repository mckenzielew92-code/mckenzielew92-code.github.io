Hotel Special Services

The Temple Inn Suites is accomodatin to those who are wanting to visit the temple or perform service for the church in various ways. We have many different types of suites to accomodate those different means of temple attendance or church service. Our rooms accomodate for those who wish to visit the temple for just a day, or those who are serving as missionaries. By staying with us you will have the luxuries of the following services:

Specialized Services:

  • Changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons
  • Low-cost short-term babysitting service for those attending temple ordinances
  • Long-term stay for missionaries including accomodations with kitchenette suites
  • Temple history themed reception and lounge
  • Wedding reception hall with catering service
  • Family search center that supports family search and ordinace work with missionaries offering help with any questions

Hotel Amenities:

  • Breakfast
  • Exercise Gym
  • Indoor Pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet & Smart TV
  • Parking
  • Temple Shuttle
  • Family History conustand and family ordinance card services
